Hiya! I'm Louis.

I'm an Aussie-American software engineer and cellist based in NYC.

Coding for me is all about the creative process, and I like making things that align with my other interests, like a musical programming language or a simulation in 3d graphics. Above all, I love using computers to solve big problems.

I'm currently working as a software engineer at Recombine, a genetics startup in the Flatiron area. To learn more about me, check out my resume or connect with me on LinkedIn.

I'm using my 20s to explore and find the experiences, people, ideas, and places that challenge me, engage me, and make me happy.

Away from the computer, I love to create things. Music is my biggest passion and I play a weekly gig with my friends at Caffe Vivaldi in the West Village.

Over the past few years, I've gotten pretty into photography, and I'm going to update this website with some more photos and a blog soon! I'm also into cooking, especially Mediterranean-style food.
